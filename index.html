About Taekwondo

Taekwondo is a Korean martial art that focuses on high kicks, fast strikes, and strong discipline. It combines combat techniques, self-defense, sport, exercise, and philosophy.

Belt System

  • White Belt – Beginner
  • Yellow Belt – Basic Techniques
  • Green Belt – Developing Skills
  • Blue Belt – Advanced Techniques
  • Red Belt – High Level
  • Black Belt – Expert

Benefits of Taekwondo

  • Improves physical fitness
  • Builds confidence
  • Enhances discipline and respect
  • Teaches self-defense
  • Develops mental focus
